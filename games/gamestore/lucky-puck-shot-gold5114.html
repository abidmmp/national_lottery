Game Procedures including how to play

Game Name: Lucky Puck Shot Gold

INTRODUCTION

These are the Game Procedures (the “Procedures”) for Lucky Puck Shot Gold (the “Game”). When You play the Game, these Procedures, the Rules for Interactive Instant Win Games ("the Rules") and the Account Terms apply. The Rules and Account Terms can be found on the National Lottery website.

Any word or term in these Procedures that has specific meaning will have the meaning given to it in these Procedures or the Rules (unless the context clearly indicates otherwise).

GAME DETAILS

Game Name: Lucky Puck Shot Gold

Game Price: £2.00

There is a 1 in 2.76 overall chance of winning a Prize on each Play of the Game. The expected prize payout percentage for this game is 70.00%.

This Game is a game of chance. The outcome of a Play in the Game is pre-determined by Camelot's Computer System at the point of purchase. You are not required to exercise any skill or judgement to win a Prize.

HOW TO PLAY AND WIN

Each Play has its own Play Number - this can be found on the Game outcome screen once the Game has been finished, in the “My Games” section of Your Account.

At the start of a Play, You are presented with a splash screen displaying the Game Name, a 'PLAY' button and the message 'WIN UP TO £50,000'. You must select the 'PLAY' button to continue.

On doing so, the Game Play Window will change and You will be presented with the Main Game Play Screen which consists of a three by three grid containing nine squares resembling an air hockey table (the “Play Grid”). The Play Grid contains three pockets at either end of the Play Grid (the “Pockets”, each a “Pocket”) Each square and Pocket contains a grey pip (Each a “Pip” or together the “Pips”) To the right of the Play Grid is a prize table consisting of seven monetary amounts, each with a corresponding colour of eight unfilled squares (the “Prize Table”).

Beneath the Play Grid are five green directional arrows (the “Directional Arrows”) and below the Directional Arrows are four blue pucks (the “Pucks”, each a “Puck”) with the words 'PUCKS LEFT' underneath (the “Pucks Left Counter”).

To the right of the Pucks Left Counter is a motif and the instructions 'REVEAL 3 OR MORE BONUS SYMBOLS IN A SINGLE SHOT TO UNLOCK THE BONUS GAME'.

At the start of Your Play, the words 'HOW TO PLAY' will be displayed to the left of the Play Grid, with a motif underneath, and a motif on the right hand side of the Play Grid, which You must select to move through each of the instructions. As You do so, a motif will appear on the left hand side of the Play Grid which You can select to return to the previous instruction.

The following seven instructions will appear at different points on the Main Game Play Screen as You navigate through the instructions:

1) YOU ARE AWARDED 4 PUCKS AT THE START OF THE GAME.

2) CLICK ONE OF THE FIVE DIRECTIONAL ARROWS TO STRIKE YOUR PUCK ACROSS THE PLAY GRID.

3) SPINNING BUMPERS WILL APPEAR IN SOME OF THE POCKETS WHICH IF HIT WILL EXTEND THE JOURNEY OF YOUR PUCK.

4) AS THE PUCK SLIDES ACROSS ANY PIP, A COLOURED SYMBOL OR A BONUS SYMBOL WILL BE REVEALED IN THAT SQUARE. THE COLOURED SYMBOLS YOU REVEAL WILL FILL THE PRIZE TABLE.

5) REVEAL A MINIMUM OF THREE BONUS SYMBOLS IN A SINGLE SHOT TO UNLOCK THE BONUS GAME. 3 REVEALED=1 BONUS GAME PUCK. 4 REVEALED=2 BONUS GAME PUCKS. 5 REVEALED=3 BONUS GAME PUCKS.

6) IN THE BONUS GAME USE YOUR PUCK(S) TO REVEAL BONUSES OR CASH PRIZES TO WIN INSTANTLY!

7) FILL A ROW IN THE PRIZE TABLE OR COLLECT AN INSTANT PRIZE IN THE BONUS GAME TO WIN!

You can select the motif to close the instructions if You wish, and return to the Main Game Play Screen.

To start Your Play, You must select one of the Directional Arrows. On doing so, a Puck will be released onto the Play Grid and begin moving around the squares on the Play Grid.

As the Puck moves across the Play Grid, each Pip within a square that it passes over will reveal a coloured symbol or a motif until the Puck enters one of Pockets. The Puck must pass over the Pip within the square in order to reveal a coloured symbol or a motif. Each coloured symbol You reveal will populate a corresponding square in the Prize Table. If You populate all eight squares for one of the monetary amounts in the Prize Table, that monetary amount and the corresponding square will animate and pulse to indicate that You have won that monetary amount.

While the Puck is moving around the Play Grid, a spinner will appear from some or all of the Pockets (the “Spinning Bumpers”, each a “Spinning Bumper”). If a Puck hits a Spinning Bumper it will extend the journey and change the direction of that Puck. That Spinning Bumper will then disappear and the Pocket it was covering will be open.

Your go ends once the Puck enters a Pocket. You must then select another Directional Arrow to release another Puck. You must continue to select Directional Arrows until You have used all of Your remaining Pucks and there are no Pucks left in Pucks Left Counter.

Bonus Game

If You reveal three or more motifs in a single shot, You will be presented with the message 'BONUS GAME' followed by the number of Bonus Pucks you will start your Bonus Game with. The Main Game Play Screen will then change to a darker screen, the Play Grid will be illuminated (the “Bonus Play Grid”) and the Directional Arrows will change from green to yellow (the “Bonus Directional Arrows”). Below the Bonus Directional Arrows will be between 1-3 white pucks (the “Bonus Pucks”, each a “Bonus Puck”) with the words 'BONUS PUCKS' displayed underneath them (the “Bonus Pucks Left Counter”).

The amount of Bonus Pucks in the Bonus Pucks Left Counter will depend on the amount of motifs You collected during Your Go.

If You collected three motifs, You will start Your Play of the Bonus Game with one Bonus Puck.

If You collected four motifs, You will start Your Play of the Bonus Game with two Bonus Pucks.

If You collected five motifs, You will start Your Play of the Bonus Game with three Bonus Pucks.

To start Your Play of the Bonus Game, You must select one of Bonus Directional Arrows. On doing so, a Bonus Puck will be released onto the Bonus Play Grid and move around the squares within the Bonus Play Grid. At the same time, Spinning Bumpers will appear in some or all of the Pockets on the Bonus Play Grid. If a Bonus Puck hits a Spinning Bumper it will extend the journey and change the direction of that Bonus Puck. That Spinning Bumper will then disappear and the Pocket it was covering will be open.

As the Bonus Puck moves across the Bonus Play Grid, each square it passes over will reveal either a coloured symbol with a '+' and number displayed inside (the “Bonus Coloured Symbols”), a Patterned Symbol with a monetary value displayed inside (an “Instant Win Prize”), a symbol with the words 'EXTRA PUCK' displayed inside (an “Extra Puck Symbol”), or a symbol with the words 'BOOST PRIZE' displayed inside (a “Boost Prize Symbol”). Any Boost Prize Symbol revealed will be displayed in a colour that matches a row in the Prize Table. The Bonus Puck will complete its journey by entering one of Pockets in the Play Grid.

If, during Your Play of the Bonus Game, You reveal a Bonus Coloured Symbol(s), those symbol(s) will populate the corresponding number of squares in the Prize Table. The number displayed on the Bonus Coloured Symbol(s) will determine how many squares will populate for the corresponding monetary amount in the Prize Table.

If, during Your Play of the Bonus Game, you reveal an Instant Prize symbol the monetary value displayed in the Instant Prize symbol will pulse to indicate that You have won that amount. A panel labelled 'INSTANT PRIZE' will then appear to the left of the Bonus Play Grid (the “Instant Prize Total”) and that monetary amount will be added to the Instant Prize Total.

If, during Your Play of the Bonus Game, You reveal an Extra Puck Symbol(s) one extra Puck per symbol revealed will be added to the Pucks Left Counter to be used when You return to the Main Game.

If, during Your Play of the Bonus Game, You reveal a Boost Prize Symbol(s) the corresponding prize amount in the Prize Table (determined by the colour of the Boost Prize Symbol) will increase in value by half of its original amount. The boosted prize amount will then change in the Prize Table.

You must continue to select Bonus Directional Arrows until You have used all of Your Bonus Pucks.

When You have used all the Pucks in the Pucks Left Counter and all the Bonus Pucks in the Bonus Pucks Left Counter (if applicable), an end message will appear displaying the prize amount You have won, if any. To end Your Play of the Game You must select the 'FINISH' button which will appear below the end message.

If You have bought a Play of this Game and have populated all eight squares of any of the prize amounts in the Prize Table and/or revealed an instant prize in the Bonus Game, You will, providing the requirements of these Procedures and the Rules are met, be entitled to a Prize as detailed in the table below:
