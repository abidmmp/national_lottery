Scratchcards

Could you scratch and win?

Scratchcards are the games that give you the chance to scratch, play and win prizes in an instant. Whether it’s a nice £25 win in time for the weekend or a jaw dropping £1MMillion reveal, a Scratchcard from The National Lottery could instantly boost your day.

You can buy Scratchcards in over 40,000 shops across the UK or add them to your online shop at selected supermarkets such as Tesco.com, Asda.com and Morrisons.com‡ (sign in may be required to search Scratchcards).
